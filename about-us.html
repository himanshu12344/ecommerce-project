History
            Flipkart was founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal 

            and Binny Bansal, both alumni of the Indian 

            Institute of Technology Delhi. They worked for 

            Amazon.com, and left to create their new company 

            incorporated in October 2007 as Flipkart Online 

            Services Pvt. Ltd. The first product they sold was 

            the book Leaving Microsoft To Change The World to a 

            customer from Hyderabad.Flipkart now employs more than 

            33,000 people.
